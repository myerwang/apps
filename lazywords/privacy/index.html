Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

Overview

LazyWords is designed as a local-first English vocabulary learning app. The current version does not require an account and does not upload learning progress to a server operated by us.

Data We Store On Your Device

The app may store learning progress, selected difficulty, character choice, game inventory, local settings, audio preferences, battle history, and anonymous local identifiers on your device. This data is used to provide app functionality and restore your local progress.

Data Collection

The current version does not include advertising SDKs, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not sell personal data.

LazyWords uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash and diagnostic information in iOS production builds. This may include crash stack traces, app version, operating system version, device model, device state relevant to the crash, timestamps, Firebase installation identifiers, and related diagnostic data. This information is used only to find, diagnose, and fix app stability problems.

LazyWords does not use Firebase Analytics and does not attach your name, email address, phone number, or free-text user identifier to Crashlytics reports.

Speech And Audio

LazyWords uses device speech and audio capabilities to play English pronunciation, example sentence pronunciation, sound effects, and background music. These features are used for learning and gameplay.

Purchases

LazyWords may offer in-app purchases in production releases. Purchases are processed by the app store platform. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

Data Export And Deletion

The app provides local controls to export learning data as JSON and to clear local learning data from the device. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored app data depending on platform behavior.

Children

LazyWords is a general vocabulary learning app and is not directed to children under 13. If future releases add account or cloud features, this policy will be updated before release.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, purchases, accounts, or cloud sync behavior changes.
