Common Topics

  • Dictionary initialization: keep enough free storage, close the app completely, and reopen it before retrying.
  • Missing words: search normally. Valid local misses can enter the protected Pending Words maintenance flow automatically.
  • YomiChan Plus: open Settings from the gear icon beside the home search field.
  • Restore purchases: use Restore Purchases on the YomiChan Plus page or in Settings while signed in with the purchasing Apple ID.
  • iCloud sync: confirm iCloud Drive is enabled for YomiChan, then use Sync Now in Settings.
  • Public profile: a nickname is permanent after initial creation. Avatar and non-empty biography changes are rate-limited.
  • Danmaku safety: tap a danmaku to view its author, report the message, or block that author on the current device.
  • Word errors: use the feedback control in the word-detail screen so the affected entry is included automatically.
